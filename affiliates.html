Frequently asked questions

Who can join?
Nurse creators, healthcare educators, NHS lifestyle content creators, revalidation bloggers — anyone with an engaged nursing or healthcare audience on social media, YouTube, or email.
How is commission calculated?
You earn 30% of the subscription price (before VAT) for every paid user who signs up through your unique link. This applies to monthly and yearly subscriptions and renewes with each billing cycle.
When and how do I get paid?
Payouts are processed monthly for approved earnings. Payment is via bank transfer or PayPal. The minimum payout threshold is £50.
How do I track my referrals?
Once you're in the programme, you get access to an affiliate dashboard in the app showing clicks, sign-ups, conversions, and your earnings in real time.
What counts as a conversion?
A conversion is counted when someone clicks your referral link, downloads the app, creates an account, and starts a paid subscription. Free users are tracked but only paid subscriptions earn commission.
Can I promote on any platform?
Yes — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook groups, email newsletters, blogs, WhatsApp. Just make sure your content complies with platform guidelines and the ASA code for affiliate disclosure.
