What Makes a Good NMC Revalidation App?

Before the comparison, a quick framework. A revalidation app needs to cover five things:

  1. CPD tracking — log hours, track participatory vs non-participatory, running totals
  2. Reflective accounts — store and organise your 5 written accounts
  3. Practice hours — confirm you've met 450 (or 900 for dual registrants)
  4. Feedback records — collect and store written feedback
  5. Portfolio export — produce something you can share with your confirmer

Most apps do the first four well enough. The gap is in how much time they save you.
