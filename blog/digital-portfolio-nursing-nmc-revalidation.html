What Is a Digital Portfolio for Nurses?

A digital portfolio is simply an electronic version of your revalidation record. Instead of paper forms, printed certificates, and hand-written reflections, everything lives in an app or online system that you can access from your phone or computer.

For NMC revalidation, your portfolio needs to hold:

  • Practice hours log: evidence of 450 hours of registered practice over 3 years
  • CPD record: 35 hours including at least 20 participatory hours
  • Reflective accounts: 5 written reflections linked to the NMC Code
  • Practice-related feedback: 5 pieces of written feedback
  • Reflective discussion record: confirmation of your discussion with another nurse
  • Confirmer sign-off: your signed revalidation confirmation

A good digital portfolio doesn't just store this stuff: it helps you build it. You log your CPD after each course or study day. You record a voice reflection after a tough shift. The portfolio organises it, totals it, and keeps you audit-ready at all times.

Why Switch to a Digital Portfolio?

1. You Always Know Where You Stand

With paper, you're guessing. Have you done enough CPD this year? Are you on track for 450 practice hours? A digital portfolio shows you real-time totals. No surprises at the three-year mark.

2. Nothing Gets Lost

Lost a CPD certificate? Forgot to print a reflection? In a digital portfolio, everything is backed up on your phone or in the cloud. Your entire revalidation history is never more than a tap away.

3. You Can Log in Two Minutes

Paper forces you to sit down at a desk. A digital portfolio works on your phone: log CPD on your break, dictate a reflection in the car park after your shift, add practice hours while waiting for handover. You capture things while they're fresh.

4. Export-Ready for Submission

When revalidation time comes, a digital portfolio lets you export everything in one go: or print individual forms: rather than hunting through files.

5. Easier for Your Confirmer

A well-organised digital portfolio makes your confirmer's job straightforward. Everything is in one place, formatted, and ready to review.

Common Questions About Digital Portfolios for Nursing

Does the NMC accept digital portfolios?

Yes. The NMC does not require a specific format. Your portfolio can be paper-based, digital, or a mix. What matters is that it contains the required evidence and can be reviewed by your confirmer and, if selected, by the NMC.

Is my data safe?

Revalidation Copilot uses encrypted storage and standard data protection practices. Your portfolio is private to you: you control what gets exported and shared.

Can I use a digital portfolio alongside paper records?

Absolutely. Many nurses start digital but keep paper certificates in a folder as backup. The app complements whatever system works for you.

What if I change jobs or move trusts?

Your digital portfolio moves with you. Unlike paper records that stay with your employer, your app-based portfolio stays on your phone. You don't lose anything when you change jobs.
