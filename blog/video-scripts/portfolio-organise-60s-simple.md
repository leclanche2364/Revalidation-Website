# Video Script: How to Organise Your NMC Revalidation Portfolio

**Format:** 60-second video with voiceover
**Tone:** Calm, direct, like a colleague explaining something over coffee
**Music:** Low-tempo, chill, no lyrics

---

## SCENE 1 (0:00–0:10)

**Visual:** Shot of a nurse sitting at a table with a laptop. They scroll through emails. Stack of papers beside them. They look at the calendar on the wall. Looks stressed.

**VO:**
Six weeks before your deadline, you open your laptop. And you realise you stopped tracking about two years ago.

---

## SCENE 2 (0:10–0:18)

**Visual:** Quick cuts of: email inbox full of certificates, a blank Word document, a calendar with months crossed out.

**VO:**
CPD certificates are scattered across your email. Reflective accounts are still unwritten. Your practice hours are a guess you cannot back up.

---

## SCENE 3 (0:18–0:25)

**Visual:** Nurse closes laptop. Leans back. Takes a breath. Then opens a notebook and starts writing calmly.

**VO:**
Your portfolio needs six things. Four hundred and fifty practice hours. Thirty-five hours of CPD. Five reflective accounts. Feedback. A reflective discussion record. Confirmation sign-off. That is the whole list.

---

## SCENE 4 (0:25–0:34)

**Visual:** Close-up of someone opening a folder on their computer. Six subfolders inside. Papers start trying to fly out. They push them back in.

**VO:**
The old way is a folder with six subfolders and a spreadsheet. It works if you have perfect discipline. But you are working thirty-seven and a half hours a week. You do not have perfect discipline.

---

## SCENE 5 (0:34–0:44)

**Visual:** Shot of a phone screen. The app dashboard is visible. A CPD counter, a practice hours bar, a list of reflections. Numbers tick up smoothly as the camera stays on the screen.

**VO:**
One app replaces all of that. Log your practice hours at the end of each month. The running total updates itself. Log your CPD when you finish a course. The app tracks the hours for you.

---

## SCENE 6 (0:44–0:51)

**Visual:** Nurse holds phone up and speaks into it. On the screen, a waveform appears. The waveform turns into a block of text. They tap save.

**VO:**
After a shift that taught you something, record sixty seconds of voice. The AI drafts a structured reflective account. You review it, tweak it, and save it. Done.

---

## SCENE 7 (0:51–0:56)

**Visual:** The phone screen shows an "Export Portfolio" button. A finger taps it. A file appears with all sections checked off.

**VO:**
When revalidation comes, you export one file. Your whole portfolio, ready to share with your confirmer or the NMC.

---

## SCENE 8 (0:56–1:00)

**Visual:** App logo on screen. Below it, App Store and Play Store buttons. The tagline: "You do the nursing. We handle the paperwork."

**VO:**
Revalidation Copilot. Free on iOS and Android. No card needed. Start today.

---
