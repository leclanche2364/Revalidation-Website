What is the NMC Code consultation? ▼
The NMC is reviewing the Code and revalidation process for the first time since 2015. The 12-week public consultation opens in September 2026 and covers AI, anti-racism, staff wellbeing, and revalidation changes.
How long does the wizard take? ▼
The wizard takes approximately 15 minutes to complete 20 questions. You can save your progress and return later.

Yes, the wizard is completely free. No credit card or account required — just enter your email to save your draft or submit directly to the NMC.

Yes. Our prep mode lets you answer draft questions based on the 7 confirmed themes now. We'll save your draft and notify you when the consultation opens in September.

No account needed. You can complete the entire wizard and download your PDF without signing up. Email is only required to save your draft or have us submit to the NMC on your behalf.
