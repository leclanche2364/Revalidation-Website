How it works

A clearer workflow for NMC revalidation

This page shows how the app supports users from setting their revalidation date to organising records and preparing them for review.

Designed around the process

Keep the workflow practical: set your date, add records over time, draft reflections, review progress, and export records when needed.

1

Set your revalidation date

Begin with the date so the app can frame the cycle and keep the timeline visible.

2

Log CPD and practice learning

Capture development activity in a more consistent way throughout the period.

3

Save feedback as it happens

Store meaningful feedback before the details are forgotten or lost.

4

Draft reflective accounts

Use notes as the starting point for stronger and clearer reflective drafts.

5

Organise evidence

Keep supporting material easier to find and easier to review later.

6

Track progress

Show what is complete and what still needs attention before renewal.

7

Prepare for review and export

Bring records together in a cleaner format for final checking.
