A common challenge

Revalidation is easier when your records are not scattered everywhere

Many nurses end up piecing together notes from phones, emails, paper forms, and old files. That makes the process more stressful than it needs to be.

✓
CPD notes spread across different places
Harder to review when your renewal date gets closer.
✓
Feedback is easy to lose over time
Useful comments get buried in messages and memory.
✓
Reflective writing often gets delayed
Drafting can feel like the hardest part when you leave it late.

Privacy and responsibility

Supportive AI, with you still in control

The app is designed to support organisation and drafting, while keeping professional review and responsibility with the user.

  • ✓Avoid entering patient-identifiable information into records and drafts.
  • ✓Review, edit, and finalise any AI-assisted content before using it.
  • ✓Use the app as a support tool for record-keeping, not as a replacement for professional judgement.
