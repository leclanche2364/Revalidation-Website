The real challenge

Most nurses leave revalidation until it becomes a crisis. That is not a character flaw — it is what happens when the process is this confusing.

You know revalidation is coming. You have known for two years. But every time you open the NMC website, the forms feel unclear, the requirements blur together, and it goes back on the list.
